Cost guide

Bus advertising cost in India

Bus advertising pricing changes based on inventory type, route density, production needs, and campaign duration. The right budget is not just the cheapest quote. It is the one that matches your target city mix, audience density, and visibility goals.

What affects the price

  • Bus format: side panel, back panel, full wrap, or interior media.
  • City quality: premium metro routes usually cost more than tier-2 city routes.
  • Duration: longer campaigns improve negotiation leverage.
  • Creative production: design, printing, and installation are separate costs in many quotes.

How brands should budget

If you are testing a new region, start with one to three cities and a focused route plan. For launches and seasonality, use concentrated bursts so the campaign feels present without spreading the budget too thin.

Useful budget bands

Local or pilot activations can begin with lower spend, while multi-city national executions require a higher media and production budget. Ask for separate line items so you can compare coverage, not just the headline number.
